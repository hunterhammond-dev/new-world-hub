About New World

New World is a MMORPG set to have a full release on August 25, 2020 being developed by Amazon Game Studios and Double Helix Games. The game is set in a supernatural land known as Aeternum. Players will have to fight the wilds and the other inhabitants of Aeternum in order to stake their claim.
